Attendance & Leave Policy

1. Work Days and Holidays

Work Days: Monday to Saturday

Off Day: Sunday

Public Holidays: Off, only if approved by management.

2. Work Time and Attendance

Start Time: All employees must start work by 10:00 AM.

Morning Meeting: Attendance is mandatory. If an employee is unable to start at 10:00 AM, they must inform their higher official with a valid reason.

Work Hours: No fixed number of hours per day, but daily assigned tasks must be completed. Failure to deliver tasks may result in a loss of pay or a deduction of half a day's pay.

Monitoring: Employees must be available on WhatsApp during work hours. Being unavailable for more than 30 minutes without notifying a higher official may result in a loss of pay for a full day or half a day.

3. Payable Leaves

Casual Leave: 1 casual leave with pay per month.

Medical Leave: 1 medical leave without submitting a medical certificate. For more than 1 medical leave, a medical certificate must be submitted. Payment for additional medical leaves is subject to management's approval.

4. Salary

Salary Credit Date: 5th of every month. If the 5th is a bank holiday, salary will be credited the next day.

Salary Calculation Period: From the 1st to the last day of each month.
